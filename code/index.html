Your knowledge in Roman history starts here.

Learn about the history of Rome from its birth as a small kingdom to its demise in the Fall of Constantinople.

Contents

Unit I

You'll learn about the myth of Romulus and Remus, the rise and fall of the Roman Kingdom, and the early years of the Roman Republic.

Unit II

You'll learn about the zenith, decline, and fall of the Roman Republic, the rise of the Roman Empire, and the Pax Romana.

Unit III

You'll learn about the stagnation and decline of the Roman Empire, its partitioning into two entities, and the gradual fall of the Western Roman Empire.

Unit IV

You'll learn about the golden age of the Eastern Roman Empire, its stagnation and decline, and its destruction at the hands of the Ottoman Turks.
